Scheduling That Works for Everyone

Whether you're a physician managing your own schedule or leading workforce planning for a multi-site system, Momentum handles the complexity automatically.

Fair Schedules, Every Time

Zero scheduling disputes

Automatically balance shift counts, RVUs, weekend rotations, and provider preferences. Every schedule is equitable and transparent—so clinicians trust the process, admins reduce complaints, and leaders improve retention.

Intelligent Automation That Adapts to You

Hours saved every week

From simple rotations to complex multi-site coverage, Momentum automates schedule generation, conflict detection, and compliance tracking. Customize the rules once, then let the system handle the repetitive work—freeing your team to focus on exceptions, strategy, and patient care.

Always Covered, Never Surprised

Automated conflict detection catches coverage gaps, overtime violations, and compliance issues before they become problems. Better schedules mean better patient access, fewer last-minute emergencies, and happier staff.
