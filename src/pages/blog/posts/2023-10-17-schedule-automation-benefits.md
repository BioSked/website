## Challenges of manual physician scheduling

Manually scheduling physician shifts can be a time-consuming and error-prone task. Healthcare facilities often struggle with multiple challenges, such as:

1. **Scheduling Conflicts:** Coordinating the availability of multiple physicians while considering their preferences, vacation time, and other commitments can result in scheduling conflicts. These conflicts can lead to understaffed shifts or overworked physicians, compromising patient care and staff morale.
2. **Inefficient Communication:** With traditional scheduling methods, communication regarding schedule changes or shift swaps can be cumbersome. This leads to miscommunication and confusion among the medical staff, potentially affecting patient care.
3. **Lack of Real-time Updates:** Manual scheduling often means that physicians are not immediately aware of any changes to their schedules. This can result in frustration for both physicians and patients.
